Chilly Chicken Momos Recipe

Ingredients

Chicken Kima
Flour
Onion (chopped)
Garlic & Ginger paste
Soy sauce
Green chilies
Capsicum
Tomato ketchup
Red chili sauce
Salt

Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare dough with flour and water.
  2. Mix chicken Kima with onion, garlic, ginger, and seasoning.
  3. Fill dough pockets with chicken mixture and steam until cooked.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, onion, capsicum, and chilies.
  5. Add sauces and toss steamed momos until coated.
  6. Serve in a plate.
